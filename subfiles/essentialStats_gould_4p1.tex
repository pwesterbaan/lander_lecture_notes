    \begin{defn*}
      A \textbf{scatterplot} is used when examining the relationship between two \emph{numerical} variables where each point represents an observation. With scatterplots, we examine the
      \TabPositions{30mm}
      \begin{itemize}
        \item \textbf{trend}: \tab general tendency of the scatter plot going from left to right
        \item \textbf{strength}: \tab strong associations have little vertical variation
        \item \textbf{shape}: \tab is the scatterplot linear or nonlinear?
      \end{itemize}
    \end{defn*}
    \vspace*{\stretch{1}}
